We believe in "no bullshit."

We don't have any crazy philosophies when it comes to our workflow. We're like any other business, we like our clients happy. We've found that they are most satisfied when they are involved in every single step of the process. If you've ever hired a contractor for some home development project that probably makes perfect sense.

We study your business

First we all meet and talk about your business, your customers, and most importantly your product. You tell us what you need from a website and we spend the next week obsessively blueprinting and researching. We then come back with a few plans of attack and then discuss how to move forward.

A bunch of mock-up designs

The most important aspect of the website is the design, even before the coding.

After we decide where to go with this project we will spend a few days coming up with various lauouts and designs.

You will then review them together and if necessary we will revivse over and over until everyone is satisfied.

Build

After we settle on the final design, we can begin the actual coding. We make sure that our websites work in all browsers, are fast and show up as one of the top Google search results.

Every project is different depending on the purpose of the website and how easily our clients want to make changes afterwards. As such, this step can take a couple weeks.

Talking money

We are trying out a subscription payment model based on goodwill and trust between us and our clients. You pay us a small percentage of the total fee at certain milestones of the project and if you feel unsatisfied at any point you can jump ship.

However, if you'd like to work with a standard down payment model that option is also open. For the most part we are pretty flexible with our payment plans... as long as we're all signing contracts!
