Different tools for every project

Some websites are updated a few times a year and others are updated as often as every day. Typically we prefer to code a website from scratch with HTML5, CSS3, and a Javascript framework, but depending on your needs we also offer popular content management systems such as WordPress and Squarespace. Coding from scratch offers more versatality in design, whereas using a CMS allows for easier content publishing later down the road.

Complimentary Training

As with any other self respecting programmer we love to keep solid documentation so that you or other future editors can look through your website and easily make changes. We keep records on every technical detail and if you'd like, we'll train you so that you have full control over your website. No added charge!

Research

Great website designs, aside from conveying important information, also need to reflect a brand's identity. The first step is for us to meet and discuss your business and pinpoint exactly what makes your business special.

Design

When a customer visits your website, we want them to know instantly that it belongs to you. This is the most important part of the process, even moreso than the actual coding. We'll come back with multiple mockups and revise until you're satisfied that we have a winning design.

Developement

After figuring out what your website is going to look like, we build your website from the ground up so that we can make sure your website loads quickly, looks great on all devices and has good SEO. These are the most important factors leading to higher search rankings. Since this part can get very technical you can rest assured that we'll be documenting every single step.

Launch

Once everything looks perfect we'll get on to launching your website. This can take a few days to make sure everything is working perfectly in terms of DNS, mail forwarding, and load speeds.
