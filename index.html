This Is How We Do It

This is how we do it.

Doing our Homework

The first step is visiting you to talk about your business and what you want on the website. We'll be looking into what defines your brand identity and personality. Then we'll analyze these factors and compile a few plans of attack for you to consider. And we'll do it all over again if you don't like any of them.

Designing Together

One of the most important aspects of the website is how it looks. When a customer visits the site, we want them to know instantly that it belongs to you. And the best people to review our designs are you and your customers. We'll come back with various layouts and mockups and you guys can tell us which ones you like. We'll revise it until you're satisfied.

We Build, you Learn?

No matter what method we use to build your website, we'll build it to the design specifications. We'll also make sure they load quickly, are responsive on every browser and device, and show up as one of the the top Google search results.
Programmers at heart, we'll document the steps so that when we're done and ready for launch, you know how to change things around to your will.
