Our Process

Prepare

After we've met and discussed what your needs are, we'll spend the next few days obsessively blueprinting and researching. We'll come back with a couple of plans of attack and then discuss how to go forward.

Design

Having decided on a direction to go towards, we'll be designing mockups for your review. Until we obtain your final approval on the design, we are happy to revise it over and over again based on your input.

Build

After we settle on the final design, we can begin the actual coding. After it's built, we'll be testing it and tweaking it until we have every pixel perfected. We'll make sure your site isn't buried deep into search engine results either!

Launch week!

Now that it's all done, we'll launch your site with your approval. But it doesn't stop there -- we don't want to just leave you all alone with just a website. We offer complementary training and explanations on what we did so if you need to make alterations after our partnership comes to an end, you're well enabled..
